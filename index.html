Why Our Program is the Perfect Fit for You

Seamless Online Learning

Our platform allows students to log in with their credentials and follow courses from Monday to Friday. Each day, you can engage in lessons at your own pace. On Saturdays, we meet in person for a few hours to discuss any challenges faced during the week and to collaborate with peers.

Flexible Study Hours

Our courses are designed to fit around your normal academic schedule, taking just 1-3 hours of your day. This ensures you can balance both your school studies and our program without any hassle.

Real-World Projects

We collaborate with real businesses, giving our students the chance to work on actual websites and projects. This hands-on experience ensures you gain valuable skills that are directly applicable in the industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the program? ▼

The program lasts for 9 months, divided into three levels. The first 3 months are for beginners, followed by 3 months at the intermediate level, and the final 3 months for advanced learners. By the end of the 9 months, participants will have the skills to become full-stack developers.

Do I need any prior experience? ▼

No prior coding experience is necessary. Our curriculum is designed to take you from a beginner to a proficient coder, starting from the very basics. We provide all the resources and guidance you need to succeed, regardless of your starting point.

How do I apply? ▼

You can apply through our website by filling out the application form with all the necessary information. Once your application is submitted, our team will review it, and you will be contacted via phone or email with the next steps. Just complete the form and wait for our team to get in touch with you.

What will I learn in this program? ▼

Our program covers a wide range of topics, from basic HTML and CSS to advanced JavaScript, front-end frameworks, and responsive design. You will also learn about version control with Git, project management, and how to collaborate effectively in a team setting.

What kind of support can I expect during the program? ▼

Throughout the program, you will receive continuous support from our instructors and mentors. This includes personalized feedback on your projects, one-on-one mentoring sessions, and access to a supportive community of peers. Our goal is to help you succeed at every step of your learning journey.
