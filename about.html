From beginner to pro:

Program Structure and Curriculum:

At TechTonicAfrica, our 9-month program guides students from basic to advanced skills, preparing them as full-stack developers. Over three levels, students first learn HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, and basic JavaScript. The second level covers advanced JavaScript, React.js, and an introduction to Node.js. In the final level, they deepen their Node.js knowledge, explore SQL and NoSQL databases, and complete full-stack projects. By the end, participants will have a comprehensive skill set for full-stack development.
